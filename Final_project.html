NLP Capstone Project

An in-depth exploration of NLP techniques using transformers, including preprocessing and analyzing SQUAD datasets.

Preprocessing

Preprocessing involved extracting questions and contexts from SQUAD datasets and using Wikipedia articles for external context.

Model Comparison

Compared CNN, RNN, and transformers for NLP tasks, highlighting their strengths and limitations.

Results

Average cosine similarities were used to evaluate transformer models like T5, GPT-2, and BERT variants for question-answering accuracy.
